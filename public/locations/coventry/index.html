Frequently Asked Questions

Where are Boombastic events held in Coventry?

All Coventry events are held at hmv Empire, 22 Hertford Street, CV1 1LF. It is right in the city centre, a short walk from Coventry train station.

What is THE 2PM CLUB?

THE 2PM CLUB is a daytime disco running from 2pm to 6pm. Four hours of 80s, 90s and 00s anthems with full club production, confetti moments, and a packed dance floor. All the energy of a night out, home by 7.

How do I get to hmv Empire Coventry?

hmv Empire is on Hertford Street in Coventry city centre, a 5-minute walk from Coventry train station. Multiple NCP and council car parks are within a few minutes' walk.

What time does THE 2PM CLUB finish?

THE 2PM CLUB runs from 2pm to 6pm. Doors open at 2pm with the party starting straight away. You will be out by 6pm and home by 7.
