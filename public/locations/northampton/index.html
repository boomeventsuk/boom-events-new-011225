Frequently Asked Questions

Where are Boombastic events held in Northampton?

We use two venues in Northampton: The Picturedrome on Kettering Road (NN1 4BN) for most events including Silent Disco, FOOTLOOSE 80s, GET READY and THE 2PM CLUB, and Cinch Stadium at Franklin's Gardens on Weedon Road (NN5 5BG) for larger 2PM CLUB events.

What events does Boombastic run in Northampton?

We run THE 2PM CLUB (daytime disco with 80s, 90s and 00s anthems), Silent Disco Greatest Hits (three-channel headphone party), FOOTLOOSE 80s (dedicated 80s night), GET READY (60s/70s Motown, Soul and Disco), and Family Silent Disco (ages 4+).

Is The Picturedrome accessible?

Yes, The Picturedrome is a ground-floor venue with step-free access. If you have specific accessibility requirements, contact us before booking and we will do our best to accommodate you.

What time do Northampton events start and finish?

THE 2PM CLUB runs from 2pm to 6pm. Silent Disco Greatest Hits and FOOTLOOSE 80s run from around 8pm to midnight. GET READY varies between afternoon matinees (2pm-6pm) and evening events (8pm-midnight). Check individual event pages for exact times.
