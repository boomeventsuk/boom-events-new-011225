Frequently Asked Questions

Where are Boombastic events held in Luton?

All Luton events are held at Hat Factory Arts Centre, 65-67 Bute Street, LU1 2EY. It is in the town centre, a short walk from Luton train station.

What events does Boombastic run in Luton?

We run THE 2PM CLUB in Luton, a daytime disco with 80s, 90s and 00s anthems running from 2pm to 6pm. Full club production, confetti moments, and a packed dance floor.

How do I get to Hat Factory Arts Centre?

Hat Factory Arts Centre is on Bute Street in Luton town centre, a 5-minute walk from Luton train station. There are several public car parks nearby including The Mall car park.

Do Boombastic events in Luton sell out?

Yes, THE 2PM CLUB events in Luton regularly sell out. We recommend booking early to avoid disappointment. Boombastic has a 95% historic sell-through rate across all cities.
