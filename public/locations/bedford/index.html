Frequently Asked Questions

Where are Boombastic events held in Bedford?

All Bedford events are held at Bedford Esquires, 60A Bromham Road, MK40 2QG. It is one of Bedford's best-known independent live music venues.

What events does Boombastic run in Bedford?

We run THE 2PM CLUB (daytime disco with 80s, 90s and 00s anthems, 2pm to 6pm) and FOOTLOOSE 80s (dedicated 80s night, 8pm to midnight) at Bedford Esquires.

How do I get to Bedford Esquires?

Bedford Esquires is on Bromham Road, a short walk from Bedford town centre and Bedford train station. There is street parking nearby and several public car parks within walking distance.
