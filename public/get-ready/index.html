GET READY

60s/70s Motown, Soul & Disco

Remember when music made you MOVE? When every song on the radio had that irresistible groove that got your feet tapping before you even knew it? GET READY takes you back to the golden era.

WHAT IS GET READY?

GET READY takes you back to the golden era of Motown, Soul and Disco. Four hours of the biggest floorfillers from the 60s and 70s. The songs that defined a generation, played loud, with people who feel exactly the same way.

From the first note of a Stevie Wonder classic to the closing bars of a Chic groove, every moment is designed to get you on your feet. This is not background music. This is a full-on celebration of the greatest era of soul and disco ever made.

THE ARTISTS

The Jacksons · Al Wilson · Stevie Wonder · The Four Tops · Rick James · Chic · Sister Sledge · The Temptations · James Brown · Diana Ross · The Supremes · The Marvelettes · Martha Reeves and the Vandellas · Ray Charles · Aretha Franklin · Marvin Gaye

THE FORMAT

SUNDAY MATINEE: 2PM - 6PM

GET READY runs as a Sunday matinee, perfect for an afternoon of dancing. No dark nights, no late finishes. Just pure, joyful dancing from 2pm to 6pm. Home in time for dinner, buzzing for the rest of the week.

We also run GET READY as evening specials for bigger celebrations. The Summer Special runs 8pm to midnight for those who want to dance into the night. Check individual events for timings.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is GET READY?

GET READY is a Motown, Soul and Disco event by Boombastic Events featuring the biggest floorfillers from the 1960s and 1970s. Artists include The Jacksons, Stevie Wonder, Chic, Diana Ross, Sister Sledge, James Brown, Aretha Franklin, Marvin Gaye and more.

What time does GET READY start and finish?

GET READY runs as both a Sunday matinee format (2pm to 6pm, home in time for dinner) and evening specials (8pm to midnight). Check individual event listings for specific times.

Where does GET READY take place?

GET READY events take place at The Picturedrome in Northampton, a stunning converted cinema venue with a huge dancefloor. The venue is centrally located with easy access from across the Midlands.
