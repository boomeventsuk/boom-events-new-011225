FAMILY SILENT DISCO

Dance together while everyone finds their perfect vibe. Three channels of family-friendly music on wireless headphones. Kids bounce to party hits. Teens groove to charts. Parents relive the classics. All on the same dancefloor.

WHAT IS FAMILY SILENT DISCO?

Have you ever tried dancing like nobody's watching... with headphones? At our Family Silent Disco, everyone gets their own soundtrack while sharing the same dance floor.

Kids can bounce to Party Hits, Teens to Charts and Trending while Parents groove to 80s/90s/00s favourites. All in the same space without compromising on music choices. Three channels means everyone in the family finds their perfect music vibe.

We carefully curate all three family-friendly channels, drawing from our experience hosting both adult decades parties with Boombastic and school sessions through The Silent Disco Project.

THREE CHANNELS, ONE DANCEFLOOR

BLUE: PARTY

Kids' Party Hits: Disney, Trolls, Moana, Baby Shark, YMCA, Can't Stop The Feeling. All the songs that get little ones bouncing.

RED: THROWBACK

Classics from 80s/90s/00s: Mr Brightside, Dancing Queen, Wannabe, Spice Girls, Take That. The songs parents know every word to.

GREEN: CHART

Recent (Clean) Chart and Trending: Sabrina Carpenter, Dua Lipa, Harry Styles. Clean versions of the biggest current hits for teens and tweens.

Switch channels whenever you fancy a change. Flip between kids hits and chart bangers. Dance together even when you are listening to different songs. That is the magic of a silent disco.

PERFECT FOR

Family Days Out

Something different the whole family will love. Ages 4 and up. Parents often enjoy it even more than the kids.

Birthday Parties

The ultimate birthday party activity. Bring your group for a 2-hour silent disco session they will not forget.

Weekend Plans

Doors at 1:30pm, party 2pm to 4pm. Done in time for the rest of the afternoon. Easy to fit into any Saturday.

HOW IT WORKS

Doors open at 1:30pm with the party channel already playing as you arrive. The full 3-channel experience runs from 2pm to 4pm. Pick up your headphones at the door, switch channels with one button, and dance together as a family. LED lights on the headphones show your channel colour, so you can see who is listening to what.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Family Silent Disco?

Family Silent Disco is an all-ages, family-friendly silent disco event where everyone wears wireless headphones and chooses from 3 channels. Blue plays kids party hits (Disney, Trolls, Baby Shark), Red plays 80s/90s/00s throwback classics, and Green plays clean chart and trending hits. Perfect for ages 4 and up.

What age is Family Silent Disco suitable for?

Family Silent Disco is designed for ages 4 and up. Younger children are welcome but may find the headphones too large. All three music channels are carefully curated to be family-friendly, drawing from experience hosting both adult decades parties and school sessions through The Silent Disco Project.

Can I book Family Silent Disco for a birthday party?

Family Silent Disco is a perfect birthday party activity. You can book individual tickets for your group through our event page. The 2-hour format (2pm to 4pm) with doors at 1:30pm is ideal for party groups. Contact us for group booking enquiries.
