Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our silent disco and party events

What is a silent disco?

A silent disco is an event where people dance to music played through wireless headphones rather than traditional speakers. Each person can control their own volume and often choose between different music channels.

How do the headphones work?

Our wireless headphones receive audio signals from our DJ equipment. Most events feature multiple channels (usually 3) that you can switch between using buttons on the headphones. LED lights on the headphones show which channel you're listening to.

What should I expect at a silent disco event?

You'll receive wireless headphones at the entrance and can dance to multiple music channels. Events typically feature different genres on each channel - pop, indie, dance, or themed decades music. You can switch channels and control volume as you like.

Are the events suitable for families?

Yes! We host many family-friendly events including daytime discos and special family sessions. These events are designed to be enjoyable for all ages with appropriate music and earlier finish times.

How do I book tickets?

Tickets can be booked through our event pages via Eventbrite or other ticketing platforms. Each event page has a direct booking link. We recommend booking in advance as events often sell out.

What happens if I lose or break the headphones?

Please take care of the headphones during the event. If headphones are lost or damaged, replacement charges may apply. Our staff will explain this when you collect your headphones.
