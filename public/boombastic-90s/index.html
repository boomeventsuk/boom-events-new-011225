WHAT IS BOOMBASTIC 90s?

BOOMBASTIC 90s is the flagship format, the night that gave Boombastic Events its name. Before the silent discos, before FOOTLOOSE, before GET READY, there was this: a room full of people who grew up on Oasis and the Spice Girls, who danced to Faithless at 2am, who knew every word to Wannabe before the first chorus hit.

This is not a nostalgia night that plays it safe with the obvious ten songs. This is four hours of deep, wall-to-wall 90s covering every corner of the decade. Britpop anthems. R&B slow jams. Euro dance floor-fillers. Boy band and girl band singalongs. Drum and bass that takes you right back. The songs you forgot you loved, mixed in with the ones you never stopped playing.
