About This Event

Northampton's most festive tradition is back for its tenth year. The Christmas Silent Disco takes over The Picturedrome with three channels of joy: Wham! and Mariah on Blue, indie anthems on Red, and dance bangers on Green. Add glowing headphones, confetti, and a crowd that knows every word, and you've got a party that's more fun than any office do. Expect hilarious headphone-off moments, mass singalongs, and the smug walk home knowing you just nailed Christmas the Boombastic way.
