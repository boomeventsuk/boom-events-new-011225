About This Event

GET READY is BACK for a Sunday Matinee Special! 💃

2pm-6pm — Ft the hits of 60s/70s Motown, Soul and Disco!

Join us for a Sunday matinee special of GET READY featuring the BIGGEST hits of 60s/70s Motown, Soul and Disco! 🪩

Our popular sell-out event is back! Come and banish the January blues and spend a Sunday afternoon with us at the Picturedrome, where we'll have an afternoon of 1960s and 70s Motown, Soul and Disco hits!

🎶 Our DJs will play the biggest floorfillers of the era including:

The Jacksons, Al Wilson, Stevie Wonder, The Four Tops, Rick James, Chic, Sister Sledge, The Temptations, James Brown, Diana Ross, The Supremes, The Marvelettes, Martha Reeves and the Vandellas, Ray Charles and more…

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Q: What music will you play?
A: A curated mix of 60s and 70s Soul, Motown, and Disco. Expect everything from "Needle in a Haystack" and "Respect" to "Le Freak" and "Good Times". 🎷
❓ Q: Is this a seated event?
A: This is primarily a standing and dancing event with a club atmosphere. There will be limited seating available around the venue for a rest between tracks. 💃
❓ Q: Can I come on my own?
A: Absolutely. Our crowd is friendly, welcoming, and united by a love for this music. 🤝
❓ Q: What is the dress code?
A: Smart casual is perfect, or feel free to dress for the era. Wear whatever makes you feel ready to dance. 🕺
❓ Q: Is this a Live DJ, not a band?
A: Yes, this is a live DJ rather than a live band, hence the price kept affordable for all. It's the same DJs that have given you some memorable Get Ready nights over the past seven years. 🎧
