SILENT DISCO GREATEST HITS

The most popular silent disco in the Midlands. 3 channels. 3 DJs. Wireless headphones. Your choice, your volume, your rules. Running strong for over 10 years.

WHAT IS SILENT DISCO GREATEST HITS?

Silent Disco Greatest Hits is the original headphone party. Everyone on the dancefloor wears wireless headphones and picks from 3 live DJ channels. Pop anthems, indie classics, or dance bangers. Switch whenever you want. Control your own volume. See who is listening to what by the colour of the lights on the headphones.

Take the headphones off for five seconds and you will hear shuffling feet, muffled singing, and someone shouting "THIS SONG!" before slamming their headphones back on. Three different songs happening at once. It is hilarious. It is brilliant. And it has been selling out for over a decade.

HOW IT WORKS

BLUE: POP THROWBACKS

Taylor Swift, ABBA, Beyonce, Dua Lipa. Massive singalong anthems and feel-good party classics. The songs everyone knows every word to.

GREEN: INDIE CLASSICS

Arctic Monkeys, The Killers, Oasis, Blossoms. Guitar-driven bangers and indie classics for those who like their tunes with a bit more edge.

RED: DANCE ANTHEMS

Calvin Harris, Faithless, Robin S, Disclosure. Pure dancefloor energy. House, dance and club classics to keep you moving all night.

Pick up your headphones at the door. Switch channels with one button. LED lights show your channel colour. Your headphones, your rules.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Silent Disco Greatest Hits?

A party where everyone wears wireless headphones and chooses from 3 live DJ channels: Pop, Indie, and Dance. You control your own volume and can switch channels whenever you like. It has been running for over 10 years with consistent sell-outs.

How do the 3 channels work at a silent disco?

Each pair of wireless headphones has a button to switch between 3 channels. Blue plays Pop, Green plays Indie, and Red plays Dance. LED lights on the headphones show which channel you are on, so you can see who is listening to what across the room.

Do Silent Disco Greatest Hits events sell out?

Yes. Silent Disco Greatest Hits has a 10-year track record of sell-out events across Northampton, Bedford, and the wider Midlands. We recommend booking early to avoid disappointment.

What should I wear to a silent disco?

Wear whatever you feel comfortable dancing in. There is no dress code. Some people dress up, some keep it casual. The only rule is to be ready to dance.
