The Story

It started in 2014 with one idea: throw a silent disco in Northampton and see if people turn up. They did. And they kept coming back.

Silent Disco Greatest Hits became the most popular silent disco in the region, now celebrating its 10th anniversary. Three DJs, three channels, one dance floor. The concept was simple, but the execution was anything but. Every detail mattered: the right venue, the right setlist, the right energy.

In 2025, we launched THE 2PM CLUB, a daytime disco format that ditches the headphones for a full sound system. Four hours of 80s, 90s and 00s anthems, starting at 2pm, home by 7. It became an instant sell-out across every city we took it to.

Today Boombastic Events runs sell-out events across Northampton, Bedford, Milton Keynes, Coventry, Luton and Leicester. Northampton remains home turf, where it all began.

Our Philosophy
